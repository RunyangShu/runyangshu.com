Welcome! I’m Runyang Shu.

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Quantitative Marketing at the University of Texas at Austin. My work studies how AI can help firms understand consumers, design better digital content, and govern platform experiences. I develop interpretable AI methods for visual and video data, with applications to product recommendation, consumer preference modeling, and advertising effectiveness. I also collaborate with technology companies, including Tencent and Wondershare, to run field experiments and study AI adoption and governance in real operational settings.

My research interests include AI for marketing, multimodal measurement, visual analytics, and field experimentation. Previously, I worked at Tencent as a research scientist focused on responsible AI.
