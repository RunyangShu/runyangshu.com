Welcome! My name is Runyang Shu.

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Quantitative Marketing at University of Texas at Austin. My work sits at the intersection of AI tool development and real-world deployment. I develop interpretable AI for for visual and video data to study product recommendation and advertising effectiveness. Working with companies(e.g., Tencent and Wondershare), I run field experiments that deploy AI tools in real operational settings.

My research interests include AI for marketing, multimodal measurement (image/video/physiological signals), and field experimentation. Previously, I worked at Tencent as a research scientist focused on responsible AI.
